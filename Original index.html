Manage Your Time

Somehow it’s already 3:00 p.m. yet you are still working on the same small task you started first thing this morning :) Most of us have been in this kind of situation and many of us find ourselves there just about every day. Despite our best effort to efficiently organised time, stay on top of our schedule and complete all of our task, we still find it hard to keep things under our control. These major problems occurred for some common time management mistakes like failing to Prioritized our important tasks or leave them in between for some other works. Ineffectively Scheduling our common tasks one of the most common pitfalls most ambitious people tend to make us to miss the time and energy they will need to complete a particular task. This behaviour is typical of a Type overachievers who think they can keep everything under control and never turn down an opportunity no matter how demanding it is.
